Asset Management Policy

Larsen & Toure Limited - Transportation Infrastructure Independent Company is committed to effectively plan, arrange, deploy, operate, maintain, repair, refurbish and dispose the assets across lifecycle sustainably to serve the needs of stakeholders and customers, optimizing the long-term return on investment.

Towards this we shall align objectives and ensure:

  • Compliance with legal, regulatory, environmental, and contractual requirements.
  • Integration with the organization's environment, health, workplace safety and quality management systems.
  • Periodic reviews and benchmarking of economic and technical performance of the assets, service providers and contractors.
  • Asset Management decisions are made by accurate and timely asset data.
  • Foster innovation through technology adoption, sustainable solutions, and continuous team upskilling via digital training and shared learning.
  • Identify and implement measures to mitigate risks while leveraging opportunities for sustainable growth.
  • Committed to continual improvement of the Asset Management System for operational efficiency.

About Us

At the heart of every successful construction project lies the strength of dependable equipment, the reliability of advanced plants, and the assurance of uninterrupted support. The Plant & Machinery Department exists to provide exactly that — a backbone of power and precision that keeps projects moving forward without compromise.

We are not just a support wing; we are an essential partner in progress. From supplying and maintaining heavy machinery to ensuring the smooth functioning of plants, our mission is to empower construction teams with tools that deliver strength, efficiency, and endurance. Whether it is an earthmover shaping the foundation of a project, a crusher plant breaking down raw material, or a batching plant ensuring flawless concrete supply — we ensure every machine runs at peak performance.

Our department thrives on foresight and responsibility. We understand that downtime is costly, delays are critical, and reliability is everything. That is why we bring together skilled expertise, advanced technology, and a spirit of dedication to keep every project on track. We take pride in supporting construction operations of every scale, from ambitious infrastructure works to complex urban developments.

Beyond machinery, we stand for trust. Every piece of equipment we supply, every plant we maintain, and every service we deliver is a commitment to quality, safety, and progress. In a world where construction never stops, we ensure the power behind it never falters.

The Plant & Machinery Department — driving construction forward with precision, power, and purpose.
